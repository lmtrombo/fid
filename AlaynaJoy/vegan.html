Tips On Being Vegan

So most people panic when they are thinking of what to eat when they are vegan...DON'T. Here are a few tips and tricks for beginner vegans to help them ease into it!

  • Turn your favorite Meats into Vegan dishes!

    There is a plethora of vegan meat options all over the place now that you can find at your local grocery store or even online on easy shops like Amazon. All you have to do is look! Now not all of these will taste exactly like your favorites but you will be able to find the ones you enjoy and can use for multiple things (ground up Impossible Burger makes ground beef).

  • Don't be afraid to try New Things

    Veganism is all about new experinces and creativity in food! Even it it is something brand new, always try anything once and then you'll know if you like it or not. There will be plenty of ingredients you have never heard of and dishes you have never tried. You may find your new favorite thing in taking a chance on something unknown.

  • Always ask, never Assume.

    There are plenty of vegans out there in the world and because of this food establishments have made or gotten more requests for vegan options than ever before. Don't be afraid to ask a server or even call ahead for their vegan options or menu. This way you can avoid sitting there with a dry salad or eating something else unsubstantial for your meal. There are hidden ingredients in so many food always double check what you are eating is vegan for environmental and health concerns as not eating vegan may make you sick once you have begun. Maybe even start a list of places in your area that have great vegan options and refer to it when you are in a bind.

  • Just because You're Vegan doesn't mean you don't Eat.

    You will be eating a lot, if not more than you were before. To recieve the nutrients from food you need to feel helthier and happier you will need to make the those vegetables the start of the show, so get second helpings, eat six meals a day and never consider french fries a meal. Always have your health at the forefront of your mind and eat, eat, eat.
